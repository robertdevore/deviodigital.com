Elevate Your Affiliate Marketing

AffiliLink is a powerful plugin for WordPress® designed to simplify and enhance your affiliate marketing efforts. By automatically replacing specified keywords with your affiliate links, AffiliLink saves you time and ensures consistent monetization throughout your website.

Why Choose AffiliLink?

  • Efficiency: Automate the tedious task of manually inserting affiliate links.
  • Consistency: Maintain uniformity in your affiliate linking strategy across all content.
  • Insightful Analytics: Gain valuable insights with built-in analytics tracking impressions, clicks, and click-through rates (CTR%).
  • User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy an intuitive dashboard that's easy to navigate and configure.

Key Features

Automatic Keyword Replacement

Define keywords or phrases, and AffiliLink will automatically replace them with your affiliate links throughout your content. This ensures that you never miss an opportunity to monetize your site.

How It Works:

  1. Add Keywords: Specify the keywords you want to monetize.
  2. Assign Affiliate Links: Link each keyword to one or multiple affiliate URLs.
  3. Automatic Replacement: AffiliLink scans your content and replaces keywords with the assigned links.

Multiple Affiliate Links per Keyword

Assign multiple affiliate links to a single keyword. AffiliLink rotates these links evenly, allowing you to:

  • Test Different Offers: See which affiliate links perform best.
  • Maximize Revenue: Increase the chances of conversions by presenting varied options.

Customizable Link Attributes

Enhance your links with custom attributes:

  • Rel Tags: Add nofollow, sponsored, or other rel tags to guide search engine behavior.
  • Data Attributes: Include custom data attributes for tracking and analytics purposes.

Detailed Analytics Dashboard

Monitor the performance of your affiliate links with real-time data:

  • Impressions: See how often your affiliate links are displayed.
  • Clicks: Track the number of clicks each link receives.
  • CTR%: Analyze the click-through rate to gauge effectiveness.

Visualize Data:

  • Tables: View detailed statistics in an organized format.
  • Charts: Interpret performance metrics with intuitive graphs.

Easy Management and Duplication

  • Duplicate Keywords and Links: Quickly replicate existing entries to save time.
  • Remove Entries: Easily delete outdated or underperforming keywords and links.
