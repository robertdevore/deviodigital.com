Plugin Pricing Updates

Oct 23, 2024 · Robert

At Devio Digital, we're always striving to provide the best value and flexibility for our customers, and today is no different. We've recently made some changes to our pricing structure, giving you more options to suit your needs and budget.

This blog post is going to outline what's new and what these pricing changes mean for you.

What's New?

We now offer three different pricing tiers for our WordPress® and WooCommerce® plugins:

  • 1 Website License: $89.95 per year

Perfect for individual site owners or small businesses who only need to use our plugin on a single website. This option provides full access to all features, 1 year of premium support, and regular updates.

  • 3 Website License: $189.95 per year

Designed for those who manage multiple websites. This plan offers great savings and flexibility, allowing you to install our plugin on up to 3 websites with the same 1-year access to updates and premium support.

  • Unlimited Website License: $289.95 per year

This is the ultimate plan for agencies or large-scale businesses. Use our plugin on as many sites as you need, with complete access to updates and premium support for a full year.

What This Means for You

If you've already purchased our plugins, your current plan remains active until the end of your subscription period. You'll continue receiving support and updates as normal.

When your subscription is up for renewal, you'll have the option to renew under the new pricing plans. This gives you flexibility to scale your plugin usage to fit your business growth.

Whether you're expanding your website count or need premium support across multiple projects, these plans ensure you can continue to benefit from all our premium features.

Why Do We Offer Annual Subscriptions?

We believe that providing ongoing updates and support is critical to helping you stay up-to-date with the latest WordPress® and WooCommerce® changes. Every year, we:

  • Release new features and improvements based on user feedback
  • Ensure compatibility with new WordPress® and WooCommerce® versions
  • Provide priority support to help you troubleshoot and maximize your plugin's value

By renewing your subscription each year, you continue to enjoy these benefits, ensuring your website stays secure and optimized.

What Happens if I Don't Renew?

Your plugin will continue to work as normal; no settings will be disabled or hidden, and every feature the plugin currently has will continue to be available.

However, you will no longer receive updates or premium support. This can leave your website vulnerable to future compatibility issues, security risks, and missing out on exciting new features.

Ready to Upgrade?

If you're interested in switching to one of our new plans before your renewal date, just reach out to our support team, and we'll help you transition seamlessly (with a nice discount applied as well).

Thank you for your continued trust in our products! We're excited about these new options and can't wait to keep helping you build amazing websites.
