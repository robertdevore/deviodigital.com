BoostBox Pro is an extension of the core BoostBox plugin, designed to provide additional powerful features for creating more effective, highly-targeted popups on your WordPress® site.

Building on the robust foundation of the BoostBox plugin, BoostBox Pro introduces new triggers, targeting options, and animations to help you better engage your visitors and increase conversions.

New Triggers

BoostBox Pro enhances your popup strategy with additional ways to trigger popups based on user behavior:

  • Exit Intent: Capture visitors' attention when they are about to leave your site by showing a relevant popup just before they close the tab or browser window.
  • On Click: Trigger popups when a user clicks on specific elements, such as buttons or links. This allows for targeted messaging that aligns perfectly with user interactions.

Trigger option select field example

Advanced Targeting Options

BoostBox Pro lets you display your popups with precision, ensuring they appear exactly where they'll have the most impact:

  • Custom Taxonomy Archives: Target specific taxonomy archives (like product categories, tag pages, etc.) to display popups relevant to those particular sections of your website.
  • Custom Post Type Archives: Show popups on the archive pages of custom post types (e.g., products, testimonials, or events), giving you more flexibility in targeting different content types across your site.

Target settings example

New Animation Effects

To make your popups more engaging and eye-catching, BoostBox Pro offers several new animation options:

  • Shake: A subtle shake effect draws attention without being intrusive.
  • Wiggle: A playful wiggle animation that makes your popup stand out.
  • Flip (Vertical & Horizontal): These flip animations smoothly reveal the popup with vertical or horizontal flips.
  • Rubber Band: Adds a stretchy, elastic-like animation for a fun, engaging appearance.
  • Swing: A gentle swinging motion that adds a dynamic feel to your popups.

Shake animation example

Responsive Design

All popups created with BoostBox Pro are fully responsive, ensuring that they display perfectly on both desktop and mobile devices. This helps provide a smooth user experience regardless of how visitors are interacting with your site.

Benefits of using BoostBox Pro

  • Enhanced Conversion Potential: With more precise targeting and dynamic triggers, BoostBox Pro allows you to deliver popups at the perfect moment, increasing the likelihood of converting visitors into leads or customers.
  • Greater Customization: BoostBox Pro provides flexibility in both how and where your popups are displayed, allowing you to better tailor your messaging to your audience.
  • Highly Engaging: The new animation effects make your popups more attention-grabbing, helping to increase visitor engagement without being disruptive.
